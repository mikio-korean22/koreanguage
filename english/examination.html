Korean examination

There are two kinds of Korean proficiency examination.
Each country might have original examinations but the exams which are recognized in the world are
"TOPIK(Test of Proficiency in Korean)" and "KLAT(Korean Language Ability Test)".
Both are approved by the Korean government, but in more detail, TOPIK is managed by the National Institute of International Education, and KLAT is managed and operated by Korea Educational Testing Service.
In addition, there are some Korean proficiency exams but in this site two of them are mainly explained.

The content of problems is a little bit different according to each level.
From TOPIKⅡ, you have to take a writing section and the level of difficulty is much higher than TOPIKⅠ.
The exam is divided into the morning section and the afternoon section and you can take TOPIKⅠ and TOPIKⅡ all to once.

About each question Each question is written by Korean.
The level of questions Grade 1 (beginner)： You can make use of the basic words for daily life, such as self-introduction, shopping, and ordering at restaurants, and understand the contents of familiar topics.
Grade 2 (beginner)： You can speak fundamental Korean for daily life, such as taliking on the phone, and in public situations like post offices and banks.
Grade 3 (intermediate)： You can live your daily life without any problems, and use various public facilities, and maintain social relationships with native speakers.
Grade 4 (intermediate)： You can use public facilities, understand news and newspapers to some extent, speak Korean required for general business.
Grade 5 (advanced)： You can speak Korean which is necessary for work in a specialized field without any problems, and understand the specific content such as politics, economy, society.
Grade 6 (advanced)： Although not as well as a native speaker, you can fluently speak Korean which is necessary for businesses in your specialized field, and tell your thought.

Cautions

show your identification

When you take exams, you will be required to show your public identification with your photo on it.
Example：passport, driver's license or your alien registration card (there are some cases your student ID or work pass that you often use is not recognized as your public identification)
If you lose your admission ticket, please contact the secretariat immediately.

What you can't you in exams

Cellphones, tablet devices, voice recorders, dictionaries, cameras
You can bring a correction tape because you will be passed out a pen for TOPIK at the test center and have to mark by it when you take the exam

A pen when you will be passed out

You don't need to bring your stationaries. You will be distributed a pen for marking on the sheet when you take the exam. Also, you can bring a correction tape to correct mistakes.

About cheating

Examples of cheating
Simple cheating
  • have a look at other people's answer
  • have a look at notes you prepared before the test
  • answer questions after the test finishes
  • cases you don't follow the instructions
  • put it on the desk or make use of unnecessary devices
  • take the exam in an illegal way
 Planned and deliberate cheating
  • make a contact with those who are in the outside on a test
  • intimidate others and supervisors with violence and high-pressure attitude
  • request a proxy exam and conduct it
  • take advantage of electric devices and share the information on a test

※ You will be punished as bellow if you cheat on a test

  • no score
  • can't take a test for 2 yeays

Others

  • You can take a test if you request a refund, and even if you do, it won't be scored.
  • You can't enter a room after a test begins. Please take some spare time and come to a test center.
  • If you feel unwell or have any other reason, please tell examiners.
  • In the case of TOPIKⅡ, you can't take a second round if you are absent from a first round.
  • You will be required to leave the classroom if you bother others.
  • You will be required to leave the classroom if you don't follow the instructions or cheat. 　In that case, you won't be scored and can't take a test for 2 years.
