What is Koreanguage？

Koreaguage was made in order to support your Korean studying. In this site, you can work out Korean problems for free.
It's classified into 3 levels, beginner, intermediate and advanced. In addition to that, you can solve problems of proverbs Korean frequently use. Each section has 10 problems and after you work out them, you can have a check how many you answered correctly.
Also even if you solve the problems, it's wasteful unless you review them.
In this site, you can have a look at explanations so please take advantage of them.
